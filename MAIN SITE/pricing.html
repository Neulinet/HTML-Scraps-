FAQ


Can I try before I buy ?

Sure! Get started right away with a free, fully featured trial with 1GB of free usage. You can try out Barricade and see if it is the right fit for your needs. No credit card is required to get started.

How will I know how much I am using ?

Within the settings section in Barricade you will be able to monitor your usage. We have built a detailed method of tracking usage and have the ability to track estimated monthly usage. You are in control of your budget at all times.

What if I don't know my usage ?

Don't worry, most people don't know much about their bandwidth usage. Get started within seconds without a credit card, and as your free trial goes on, we will recommend an appropriate plan based on your usage during that period. We'd rather you get protected first, learn to love the product second and lastly select a plan that works for you.

What do you mean by ‘usage’ ?

We analyze all traffic in and out of your servers/containers. This is how we learn about normal behavior and identify suspicious patterns. The more traffic you have, the higher the bandwidth, the bigger your usage. Barricades' plan grow with your needs, as your business grows and focuses on making sure you are in control of your budget at all times, you will never be overcharged for usage.

What happens if I go over my usage ?

We strongly believe in transparency and communication. When you reach 80% of your usage we'll get in touch. When you then reach 100% we'll get in touch again, and finally we will allow you to go 20% above (120%) your plan's allowance at which point we will pause the data collection and contact you again. If you don't want to upgrade your plan, the service will resume at the beginning of your next billing cycle.

What if I want to change plan ?

You can change plan at anytime. Upgrade or downgrade your plan from within Barricade whenever you like. Plans are charged on a monthly basis and are renewed at the start of the next month.

How do I cancel my account ?

You can cancel your account anytime through the control panel, under the settings tab.
